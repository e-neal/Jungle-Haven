Small Business Overview

A Jungle Bar
Experience Like No Other

Jungle Haven is a service-based business that operates as a jungle-themed bar. It offers a variety of cocktails while providing live cocktail preparation shows, turning drink service into pure entertainment.

The business focuses on creating a unique, immersive experience that combines atmosphere, performance, and quality beverages — making every visit a story worth telling.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create refreshing, jungle-inspired drinks that combine bold flavors, creativity, and positive vibes, giving customers a fun and memorable experience with every sip.

Our Vision

To become a recognized drink brand known for innovation, energy, and tropical inspiration — a name synonymous with vibrant Belizean culture.

Proposed Location

Set in
Paradise

Placencia Village is an ideal proposed location for Jungle Haven because it attracts a steady flow of tourists and locals who are seeking entertainment, nightlife, and unique experiences.

The village is known for its beachfront bars, resorts, and relaxed but vibrant atmosphere, which aligns perfectly with a jungle-themed immersive bar experience.

Placencia has a walkable main area where bars and restaurants benefit from high foot traffic, especially in the evenings. The mix of international tourists, locals, and resort guests creates a diverse customer base with real spending power for specialty drinks.

Roadmap

Our Goals

Building something extraordinary, one milestone at a time.

Short-Term Goals

  • Within 2 Months

    Collect feedback from 20 customers to improve taste, pricing, and packaging.

  • Within 3 Months

    Offer one seasonal fruit drink based on what is locally available in Belize.

  • Within 5 Months

    Sell drinks at one additional location — a school event, market, or roadside stand.

Long-Term Objectives

  • Within 3–5 Years

    Expand to multiple locations across Belize by increasing profit margins.

  • Within 4 Years

    Build a strong, recognized brand through consistent social media advertising.
